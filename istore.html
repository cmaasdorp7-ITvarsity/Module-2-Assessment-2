iSTORE


iStore is a dedicated hub for Apple products and accessories. Our first store opened in V&A Waterfront in 2005 and since then we've grown to 25 retail stores which are conveniently situated across the country as well as an online store, making iStore the largest APR in Southern Africa.

iStore offers the best possible Apple experience through expert advice and a wide range of exclusive services that ensure you get the most out of your Apple products for your personal, business and education purchases and upgrades.
